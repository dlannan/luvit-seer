  1. Our process

    • Planning
    • Content
    • Technical Design
    • Development
    • Delivery

    Planning & Research

    Initial meeting

    We discuss your goals and ideas and the Web Design Plan helps you choose what you want.

    Requirements analysis

    An outline of your goals, target audience, requirements, detailed feature requests and custom components.

    Project charter

    Defines the project, collates target information and serves as a reference throughout the project.

    Contract

    Defines roles, copyright, and financial points. Includes payment terms, project closure clauses, termination clauses, copyright ownership and timelines

    Content

    Information Architecture & Site map

    We work closely with you to develop an organisational flow and structure that best suits your business.

    Branding & Visual Identity

    We create a style guide, including color schemes, typography and web elements, from your existing branding or from scratch. If required, logo design is started here

    Content Sourcing

    We provide custom and COTS (Commercial Off The Shelf) content sourcing to reduce costs, and maintain quality. We use local content generators for important custom content.

    Technical Design

    Mockups and AGILE

    Using AGILE process techniques we ensure the customer is involved in a phased, iterative design process.

    Working prototypes

    We will provide working prototypes as early as possible. Then improve upon the prototypes every development cycle.

    Custom Components

    Using our unique modular system we can identify and develop any custom modules that will be specific to your business.

    Development

    Framework

    We can provide Admin and CMS capabilities if needed for your own content control and development.

    Website Deployment

    We turn the finalized prototypes into a completely functional website. You work with us to ensure the final output is what you expected.

    Testing & QA

    We verify functionality of the website. Do final cross-browser and platform testing. We optimize the site by compressing files, minizing css and js files, optimize image sizes. And we QA the site with our Beta-testers for final validation.

    Delivery

    Hand over

    We deploy the final website to your host and set a launch date.

    Training & documentation

    Training and Dcoumentation will be prvided where necessary.

    Maintenance plan

    We create a maintenance plan with you that suits your business and your budget.

      Our default features

    • Responsive web design

      Our websites are responsive and will look great no matter the platform they are viewed on.

    • Customization

      We can provide tailored customizations for your business

    • Browser compatibility

      We support all the common modern browsers on mobile, tablet and desktop PC.

    • Analytics

      Our specialized hosting system features modern analytics leveraging modern machine-learning techniques.

    • Small and Fast

      Our unique server system has a very small resource footprint (less than 20MB without content). And it performs at very high rates - our system can handle 1 million requests per second (if needed).

    • Quick Development

      For customers that need unique solutions, our scripting system means we can build it sooner, and to a high quality level.
